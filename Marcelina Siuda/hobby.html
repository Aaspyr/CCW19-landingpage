My hobby - things that I love

At this place I would like to show what I'm interested in.
What you find on this page are things that I really love.

Surfing

I have been surfing just for 2 years now, but it is absolutely my true love and passion. I’m all in, head over heels.When I'm catching wavesI don’t care about another thing in the world. I'm in love with surfing and the ocean.

Animals

I love animals since I was a little girl. I was blessed to live in the countryside with many animals and birds in my backyard, which helped me grow up with a true love and care for the animals. I love all animals, but my favourite are dogs and guinea pigs.

Food

"There is no sincerer love than the love of food" - I'm totally agree with this statement. I love discovering new flavors and places where I can eat something that I have never tried.

Travelling

Traveling reminds me that my life isn't the only one I could live and that in the grand scheme of things, I'm pretty unimportant to this world we live in. People get so caught up in their daily lives and forget that they can be somewhere or someone else tomorrow if they'd like.

Biology

Biology is inherently fascinating. From a very young age I have always loved reading and learning about science. Biology is unique because of the complexity of living things and how they interact with each other and the environment.

Art

I love art and think it is entirely necessary to incorporate into everyday life. Art, for me, has taught me so much. I have begun to understand, through my art in its various mediums and forms, my life.
